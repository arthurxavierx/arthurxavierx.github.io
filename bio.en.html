Arthur Xavier is an artist and composer based in Belo Horizonte and Brasília, Brazil. A former Software Engineer and bachelor in Computer Science, nowadays Arthur studies Composition at the Federal University of Minas Gerais, Brazil and has a major interest on interactive arts, sound art and electroacoustic music.

They believe in art as a tool for subjective and political liberation, and on its power of mobilizing attention, reflection and action through sensible experiences. Arthur mainly investigates the questions of subjective relations with work, technology, time and memory. Makes art to stop time, to feel present, and to leave their own head.
